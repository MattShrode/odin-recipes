Buffalo Chicken

Description

This slow cooker buffalo chicken is juicy, spicy, and simple! Onion and boneless, skinless chicken breasts are covered in a buffalo sauce mixture for a healthy and ultra versatile protein. Great in sandwiches, on salads, and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 large, boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 3/4 cup Frank's Red Hot Wings sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 3/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/8 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp dried dill
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
